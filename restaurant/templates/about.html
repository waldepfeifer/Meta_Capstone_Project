About Us

Nestled in the vibrant city of Chicago, Illinois, Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant, dedicated to presenting traditional recipes with a contemporary twist.

Our culinary maestros draw inspiration from the rich tapestry of Italian, Greek, and Turkish cultures, curating a seasonal menu of 12-15 exquisite dishes.

Immerse yourself in our rustic and inviting ambiance, where each visit promises a relaxed dining experience at moderate prices, perfect for any time of day.

Little Lemon is the vision of two Italian brothers who journeyed to the United States to fulfill their dream of owning a restaurant.

Guided by family recipes and Mario's culinary expertise acquired in Italy, our menu reflects a blend of tradition and innovation.

Meanwhile, Adrian spearheads our marketing efforts, championing the expansion of our menu to include diverse Mediterranean cuisines beyond the confines of classic Italian fare.
